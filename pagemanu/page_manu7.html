AMERICANO AND PASTRAMI SANDWICH

สิ่งที่คูณต้องเตรียม

INGREDIENTS
  • For the Americano
  • 200 ml / 6.8 oz of water
  • 6 bagels
  • bunch of salad shoots
  • capsules of Grand Cru Arpeggio
  • For the sandwich
  • 250 g / 9 oz of coleslaw
  • thin slices of pastrami (cured beef)
MATERIALS
  • oven
  • mixer or blender
  • Ritual Mug

ขั้นตอนการทำ

For the sandwich:
  • Cut the bagels in two, spread on the coleslaw, the salad shoots and the pastrami then close the sandwich and push down firmly
For the Americano:
  • Pour 2 capsules of Arpeggio into a large glass and add the hot water
